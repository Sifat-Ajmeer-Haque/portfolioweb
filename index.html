01 — About

Turning Complex Systems
into Actionable Impact

I am Sifat Ajmeer Haque, an Industrial and Production Engineer from Rajshahi, Bangladesh, currently working as Assistant Officer — Production & Planning at Akij Plastics Limited, one of Bangladesh's leading manufacturing conglomerates. I completed my B.Sc. in Industrial and Production Engineering (IPE) from the Department of IPE, Jashore University of Science and Technology (JUST) in 2023.

My research spans Industry 4.0 adoption barriers, lean manufacturing, green supply chain management, and blockchain-enabled traceability. My undergraduate thesis was published in The TQM Journal and I have co-authored paper published in the Benchmarking: An International Journal (both Emerald Publishing), accumulating 30+ scholarly citations. A manuscript on blockchain for RMG supply chain transparency is currently under peer review. I have also delivered oral presentations at IEOM and ICMERE conferences and served as a Technical Paper Reviewer for IEOM conferences in Dhaka (Bangladesh), Bangkok (Thailand), and Cape Town (South Africa).

In practice, I led the implementation of SMED (Single-Minute Exchange of Die) at Akij Plastics, achieving a 60% reduction in die changeover time and USD 4,320 in annual savings — recognised with a Key Employee Award. My research interests lie at the nexus of smart manufacturing, sustainable supply chains, and digital transformation. I am seeking a PhD scholarship opportunity where I can contribute meaningful, application-driven research to a world-class programme.
